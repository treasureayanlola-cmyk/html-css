Frequently Asked Questions

Product & Materials

What materials are your tote bags made from?

The majority of our totes are made from **100% GOTS-certified organic cotton canvas** or a durable recycled polyester blend. We always specify the exact material on the individual product page for full transparency.

How much weight can your totes hold?

Our standard canvas totes are rigorously tested and designed to safely carry up to **20 kg (44 lbs)** of weight. We use double-stitched seams and reinforced handles for maximum durability, perfect for groceries or library books!

Are your printing inks eco-friendly?

Yes! We exclusively use **water-based, non-toxic, and vegan-friendly inks** for all our designs. This ensures both vibrant colors and a minimal environmental impact.

Care & Maintenance

How should I wash my tote bag?

For the best results and to preserve the print, we recommend **spot cleaning** first. If a full wash is necessary, machine wash on a **cold, gentle cycle** and air dry flat. Avoid using a machine dryer, as this can cause shrinkage and fading.

Did my cotton tote bag shrink?

As with most natural fibers, some minor shrinkage (typically less than 5%) may occur if washed in warm or hot water. Always follow the cold wash instructions provided on the care label to minimize shrinkage.
